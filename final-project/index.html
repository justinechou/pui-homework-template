Hi there! I'm Justine

I'm a Master of Human Computer Interaction student at Carnegie Mellon University graduating in August 2024. In June 2023, I graduated from the University of California, Irvine, with a BS in Informatics with a Specialization in Human Computer Interaction and a Management Minor.
I'm a product designer who crafts digital, user friendly products & experiences with the purpose of social good. I believe that technology should be equally accessible for people of all backgrounds, and therefore construct impactful solutions with a human centered approach by understanding my users' needs, wants, and challenges.
When I'm not designing, I enjoy drawing in my sketchbook, cooking new recipes, practicing the piano, and playing volleyball & badminton.
